🛡️ General Prevention Guide

Best practices to keep your crops healthy year-round

💧

Proper Irrigation

Avoid over-watering. Use drip irrigation to keep leaves dry and reduce fungal growth.

🌀

Crop Rotation

Rotate different crop families each season to break pest and disease cycles naturally.

🌱

Resistant Varieties

Plant certified disease-resistant seed varieties available from state agricultural departments.

🔬

Soil Testing

Test soil pH and nutrients regularly. Balanced nutrition reduces plant susceptibility to diseases.

🦟

Pest Monitoring

Use sticky traps and pheromone traps for early pest detection. Scout fields twice per week.

♻️

Sanitation

Remove and destroy infected plant material. Clean tools between plots to prevent spread.
